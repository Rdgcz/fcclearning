Breaking: Local Cat Elected Mayor
Whiskers Wins Landslide Victory on Nap-Time and Snack Platform
By John Smith

In a stunning political upset, the town of Sleepy Hollow has elected a ginger tabby named Whiskers as its new mayor. The feline candidate, running on a powerful platform of mandatory afternoon naps and a dramatic increase in tuna distribution, won by a landslide, capturing 98% of the vote from both human and local wildlife constituents.

Campaign manager and owner, Mr. Higgins, stated that Whiskers's straightforward, no-nonsense approach to solving complex issues—primarily by sitting on important documents—resonated with a weary electorate. His first official act is expected to be the declaration of a national bird-watching holiday.
